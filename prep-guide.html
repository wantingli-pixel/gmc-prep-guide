Step 1 — Brand Onboarding Form

Fill this in once per brand. It tells the tool how to write titles and descriptions for this brand.

📝 Open Brand Onboarding Form
What the Form Asks
🏷️ Brand Name e.g. La Mer, Clinique, Kulanikinis
🧴 Industry e.g. Skincare, Haircare, Footwear
📍 Target Location e.g. Australia, United States — used for keyword volume data
✍️ Brand Positioning 1–2 sentences on what the brand is and who it's for
🔢 Title Structure Order of elements, e.g. Brand + Product Name + Key Benefit + Type + Size
📌 Special Rules Optional — one rule per line, e.g. "Never use the word 'luxury'"
Tips for Tricky Fields
💡
Brand Positioning Search the brand name in ChatGPT or Perplexity in AI search mode — copy the summary as your positioning.
🔢
Title Structure Define the order of what goes in the title. Include "Size (if relevant)" at the end for variant products.
📌
Special Rules Add any brand-specific restrictions here. These are injected directly into the AI prompt — be specific.
✅
Already registered? Update here instead If the brand was onboarded before, no need to re-submit the form. Edit the brand's row directly in the Brand Registry Sheet →

Column name What to fill in
